LEAVE POLICY — HR Department

1. Annual Leave
   - All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
   - Leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month of service.
   - Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days to the next year.
   - Leave beyond the carry-forward limit will be forfeited on December 31st.

2. Sick Leave
   - Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year.
   - A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
   - Unused sick leave cannot be carried forward.

3. Parental Leave
   - Maternity leave: 26 weeks of paid leave for birth mothers.
   - Paternity leave: 2 weeks of paid leave.
   - Adoption leave: 12 weeks of paid leave.

4. Leave Application Process
   - Submit leave requests at least 5 business days in advance for planned leave.
   - Emergency leave must be communicated to the manager within 2 hours of the start of the workday.
   - Approval is at the discretion of the reporting manager, considering team workload.

5. Leave Without Pay (LWP)
   - Available when all paid leave is exhausted.
   - Requires approval from both the reporting manager and HR.
   - LWP exceeding 30 days may affect employment benefits.
