EMPLOYEE BENEFITS POLICY — HR Department

1. Health Insurance
   - Comprehensive health insurance covering employee, spouse, and up to 2 children.
   - Coverage amount: Up to $500,000 per year.
   - Includes hospitalization, outpatient care, dental, and vision.
   - Pre-existing conditions covered after 1 year of continuous employment.

2. Retirement Benefits
   - 401(k) plan with company match up to 6% of base salary.
   - Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years.
   - Employees can adjust contribution percentage at any time.

3. Professional Development
   - Annual learning budget of $2,000 per employee.
   - Covers courses, certifications, conferences, and books.
   - Must be pre-approved by the reporting manager.
   - Reimbursement within 30 days of submitting receipts.

4. Wellness Program
   - Gym membership reimbursement up to $100/month.
   - Mental health support: 6 free counseling sessions per year.
   - Annual health check-up fully covered.
   - Wellness days: 2 additional days off per year for mental health.

5. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
   - 24/7 confidential counseling hotline.
   - Financial planning assistance.
   - Legal consultation (up to 3 free sessions per year).

6. Commuter Benefits
   - Public transit subsidies up to $150/month.
   - Parking allowance for office commuters: $100/month.
   - Bicycle purchase assistance program.

7. Other Perks
   - Free meals at the office cafeteria.
   - Company-sponsored team outings (quarterly).
   - Employee referral bonus: $2,000 per successful hire.
   - Birthday and work anniversary gifts.
